About the Project

Our project

We completed this project as a capstone project for DS 3001: Introduction to Data Science at WPI. We first selected the dataset we were using from Kaggle. Then, we cleaned the data and removed bad entries. For the analysis part of the project, we considered regions and filtered out individual cities to make the results less noisy. We hoped to understand avocado prices and consumption by region in the United States.

Data Analytics

Number of Avocado Bags Sold by Size Per Region

The size of an avocado does not indicate its ripeness or quality. Since the avocados seed grows with the fruit, the seed-to-fruit ratio will always be close to the same. Small avocados are the perfect size for on-the-go snacks, while larger avocados are best used for family sized portions.

Observations

  • Most regions purchased small avocados
  • Exception being the West purchasing about 40% large avocados

Recommendations

  • Increase the price of Small Avocados
  • Decrease the price of Large Avocados
  • Should help increase overall sales and revenue

Conventional vs Organic by Region

Organic foods have gained popularity in the recent years. By complying with the standards of organic farming, companies can provide a different quality product to a consumer. It is important to understand the most popular types of avocados in a region in order to best market towards that region in the future.

Observations

  • Conventional avocados are more popular than their organic counterpart
  • Northern New England (Vermont, New Hampshire, Maine) purchased nearly 3x the number of organic avocados compared to other regions

Recommendations

  • Increase their production of conventional avocados in order to optimize their revenues
  • Decrease the price of conventional avocados to increase their popularity
  • Organic foods have gained popularity - target this market!

Region vs Average Price vs Percentage of Total Bags Sold

In order to price products effectively, a company needs to understand the average price of its product in a certain region. From there, they can go and analyse other trends in that region to determine the optimal price for a product.

Observations

  • Did not find a correlation between average price and total number of bags sold
  • The West region purchased the most bags of avocados
  • Northern New England, the Plains, and WestTex-New Mexico purchased the fewest number of avocado bags.

Recommendations

  • Avocado marketers in the Northern New England would benefit from a decreased price
  • West and South Central region can increase their pricing because of high consumption
  • Southeast region is doing a great job!

Time vs Average Price per Region

Companies must understand the price at which to sell their product during different time periods in a year. For instance, if it is more expensive to grow and ship avocados in the winter, the price must be raised.

Observations

  • The price of avocados tends to climb from July to November
  • The price of avocados remains more stable from January to July
  • Notice the price spike in 2017

Recommendations

  • Consumers can use this data to understand avocado pricing during the year
  • Consumers should buy more avocados during January and July
